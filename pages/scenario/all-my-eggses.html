About the Client

All My Eggses Live in Texas is an eccentric family-owned breakfast restaurant chain with 47 locations across Texas. Founded by Emmett Hutchins III, they’re famous for their over-the-top Texas pride, punny menu items, and dad-joke-fueled marketing.

They’ve hired Bat City Collective to build a promotional video game that captures the spirit of Texas road trips. They want something their customers can play on the tableside kiosk systems while waiting for their “Eggs-traordinary Breakfast Platter.”

Their goal? Customer engagement, brand awareness, and sharing their love of Texas with the world.
