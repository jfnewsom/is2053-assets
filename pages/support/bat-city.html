About the Studio

Welcome to Bat City Collective — Austin’s premier indie game studio. Founded in 2019 by developers who escaped the crushing world of AAA game studio crunch culture, Bat City has one mission: make great games without burning out.

Our studio specializes in retro-style games, text adventures, and nostalgia-driven experiences. We’re 15 people working out of a converted warehouse in East Austin.

“Our company tagline says it all: Same Bat-code. Same Bat-channel. We believe great games are built brick by brick, with patience, craftsmanship, and a whole lot of coffee.”

Danny Cavanaugh
Lead Developer, Bat City Collective
Company Culture

Our manifesto is simple:

  • Quality over quantity — Ship fewer games, but ship them right
  • Sustainable pace — No death marches. Go home at 5.
  • Weird is good — The best ideas sound dumb at first
  • Everyone codes — Even the Creative Director pushes commits
  • Bats are cool — The Congress Avenue Bridge colony is a tourist attraction for a reason
Your Role
