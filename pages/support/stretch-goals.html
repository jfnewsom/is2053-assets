Extra Credit Through Codewars Challenges

Codewars is a platform where you can solve coding challenges (called "kata") to sharpen your skills. For this course, completing kata earns you extra credit points toward your final grade.

This is 100% optional. It's for students who want extra practice or need to boost their grade.

Getting Started with Codewars
  1. Create a free account at codewars.com
  2. Choose Python as your language
  3. Solve kata at 8 kyu, 7 kyu, or 6 kyu difficulty (easiest to harder)
  4. Take a screenshot showing your completed kata and your honor points
  5. Submit the screenshot to the Stretch Goals assignment in Canvas
Extra Credit Based on Honor Points

Extra credit is based on your total Honor points earned on Codewars. Higher honor = more extra credit!

Honor Points Extra Credit Approx. Effort
25+ Honor +1% ~10 easy kata
50+ Honor +2% ~20 easy kata
100+ Honor +3% ~35-40 kata
200+ Honor +4% Dedicated practice
300+ Honor +5% (MAX) Serious commitment!
Requirements for Extra Credit
  • Only Python solutions count (no JavaScript, Ruby, etc.)
  • Only kata at 8 kyu, 7 kyu, or 6 kyu difficulty (harder kata are beyond course scope)
  • You must submit by the last day of classes
  • Screenshot must show your username, honor total, and completed kata
  • Do NOT use AI to solve kata — the point is to practice yourself!

Start with 8 kyu kata! Search for tags like "Fundamentals," "Strings," "Basic Language Features," or "Mathematics." These align well with what we're learning in class.
