Weekly Cadence

Summer 2026 runs as a 10-week compressed term from Tue May 26 through Tue Aug 4. Each module is 14 days. Modules 1–4 follow the same cadence: two BookEx exercises, two BUILD/ENHANCE labs, and a MASTER lab. Module 5 is compressed to a single BookEx and two labs (BUILD + MASTER), with no Lab 5.3, since Chapter 11 is dropped from the course.

How Module 5 Differs

Module 5 is the only module that does not run the full BUILD/ENHANCE/MASTER three-lab cadence. The original course design had a Chapter 11 (Inheritance) MASTER lab, but Chapter 11 was dropped from the course. What remains is Chapter 10 (Classes and Objects) split across two labs:

  • Lab 5.1 (BUILD) — You build the City class from scratch and refactor your Module 4 game to use City objects.
  • Lab 5.2 (MASTER) — You build the Player class against a provided lab-5-2.py, completing the full object-oriented version of the game.

Lab 5.1 keeps the same Friday deadline (Day 5) as every other Lab X.1. Lab 5.2 lands on Wednesday Day 10 (where Lab X.2 would normally land in other modules), with the time that would have been Lab X.3 absorbed into the Module 5 exam window.
